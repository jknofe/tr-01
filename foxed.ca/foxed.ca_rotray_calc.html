Rotary Compression Calculator
What is it?

The Rotary Compression Calculator is a basic calculator designed to be used with the Twisted Rotors TR-1 or the RotaryCompressionTester RCT-V5 testers. Input pressure readings for the three faces of the rotor, along with the altitude and cranking RPM of the test. A normalized pressure reading is displayed below, giving the actual compression.
